### 1. Data Collection and Usage
AI Summarizer ("the Extension") is designed with your privacy in mind. 
- **Active Tab Content:** The Extension only reads the content of the web page you are currently viewing **when you explicitly click the "Summarize" button**.
- **No Background Tracking:** We do not track your browsing history, clicks, or any other background activity.

### 2. Third-Party Services
To generate summaries, the text extracted from the active web page is securely transmitted to our backend proxy server, which then forwards it to the [OpenRouter API](https://openrouter.ai).
- The data sent is limited exclusively to the text of the webpage and its language tag.
- Neither our server nor OpenRouter retains or trains models on the submitted text.

If you have any questions or privacy concerns, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/iscopan/ai-summarizer).
